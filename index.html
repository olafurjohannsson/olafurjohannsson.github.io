About me

As a seasoned Software Engineer with over a decade of experience, I specialize in developing cutting-edge software solutions across various industries, including Air Traffic Management, ticketing systems, and more. I currently serve at Tern Systems, where I contribute to mission-critical systems that support Iceland's vast airspace management. My work includes the design and development of next-generation tools like Polaris, an advanced aircraft surveillance display for air traffic controllers, and Air Archive, a web-based platform for replaying and analyzing surveillance data.

Prior to this, I honed my expertise through roles in dynamic and fast-paced environments, including freelance work at Leiðir Verkfræðistofa and Venuepoint A/S. My diverse skill set spans multiple programming languages and platforms, such as C++, Python, JavaScript, C#, and MySQL, with extensive experience in system architecture, web-based applications, and database management. Throughout my career, I have focused on delivering high-quality, reliable solutions by leveraging advanced technologies like Qt, OpenGL, and cloud infrastructure to meet the evolving needs of clients and industries.
